Curry Recipe

This delicious and creamy coconut curry blends the warmest of spices and hearty ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 1 14-oz. can coconut milk
  • 3 tbsp. garam masala
  • 3 carrots, chopped
  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 inches ginger, minched

Steps

  1. Heat large frying pan or saucepan on medium heat.
  2. Add 2 tablespoons olive oil and wait 30 seconds.
  3. Add carrots and fry until brown, stirring occassionally.
  4. Add garam masala, onion, garlic, and ginger. Fry until fragrant and garlic is browned.
  5. Add peppers and cook for 10 minutes.
  6. Finally, add coconut milk and cook for 5 additional minutes. Serve hot with rice.
