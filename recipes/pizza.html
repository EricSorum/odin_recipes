Pizza Recipe

This is the simplest, and most delicious recipe for pizza you'll ever see!

Ingredients

  • 1 sixteen inch premade pizza crust
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tabsp oregano
  • 2 tsbp melted butter
  • 2 c grated mozzerella cheese
  • 1 salami sausage
  • 2 tsbp chili pepper flakes

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 450 degrees fahrenheit.
  2. In a small bowl, combine tomato paste, garlic, oregano, and melted butter. Mix thoroughly.
  3. Spoon sauce onto premade pizza crust, creating a thin, even coat.
  4. Beginning around the edges, sprinkly mozarella cheese evenly around pizza.
  5. Thinly slice sausage and place event around pizza as desired.
  6. Bake pizza in preheated oven for 14 minutes, or until cheese is browned.
